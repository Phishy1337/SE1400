Competitive Analysis of Other Photographer

Annie Leibovitz's Portfolio Site is a prime example in the photography industry.

Strengths:

  • High-quality image display with optimal loading
  • Clear navigation and organization of work by category

Areas for Improvement:

  • Limited mobile responsiveness
  • Minimal information about services and pricing
  • Could benefit from more frequent content updates
